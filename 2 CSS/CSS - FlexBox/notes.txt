FlexBox
Arrange items in container
- No Floats
- Responsive
- Positioning child elements
- Order of elements can be changed

- Alter item to fit in containers available space
- Works in both directions(hor and ver) "main" and "cross" axes
- Works well for small sizes (grid works better for large)

Properties
display: flex | inline-flex
flex-direction: row | collumn (will flip direction of main and cross axis)
flex-wrap: wrap | nowrap | wrapreverse
flex-basis: <length> (width for items)
>justify-content: flex-start | flex-end | center (align content in main axis)
align-self: flex-start | flex-end | center
> align-items: flex-start | flex-end | center (align content in cross axis)  
align-content: flex-start | flex-end | center (cross axis) align content with multilines
flex-grow: <number>
flex-shrink: <number>
flex: <integer>
order: <integer>
